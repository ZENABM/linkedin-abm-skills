Ad-format best practices

From ZenABM's 2026 LinkedIn ABM Benchmarks Report (2,828 B2B SaaS ads). Winners share a specific offer + real people + an unmissable CTA. Lead with TLAs — ~77% cheaper per click than image ads.

Image ads — CTR 0.42% · CPC $13.23

Do: a specific offer (FREE/$/deadline); real people with names, not stock; one bold high-contrast CTA; mobile-first, uncluttered.
Avoid: logo/stock as hero; text-heavy UI dumps; vague "Learn More".

Thought Leader Ads — CTR 2.68% · CPC $3.06

Do: 1st-person ("I…"); open with a pain point; link in the bottom 25%; 1,000–1,500 chars, specific metrics, different credible authors.
Avoid: corporate "we"; link mid-post; hashtag spam; the same post across personas.

Video ads — CTR 0.24% · CPC $15.61

Do: hook in 3s; under 30s; your own captions; a named SME; use for warm/retargeting.
Avoid: cold-click reliance; over-funding vs TLAs; long intros.

Text ads — set-CPC, cheap reach

Do: punchy headline + one CTA; an emoji to break the sidebar pattern; test variants; always-on.
Avoid: expecting volume; giving them a click budget.
